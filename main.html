Here's a view of Bridge Captain's main screen during bidding. Bids and alerts show up on "the table" while your bids are entered with a familiar bidding box.

After you finish playing the hand, the hand is then replayed in the "Closed Room" with Bridge Captain playing all four hands. The Conventions cards are switched so that your teammates in the Closed Room (E-W) will play your CC. It takes just a few seconds before the results are then displayed. In this example, it took less than 10 seconds.
