Word Captain is similar to the Spelling Bee word game popularized by the New York Times. The goal is to make as many words as possible. Each word must be at least 4 letters long and contain the center letter. Only the 7 given letters may be used. If you make a word that uses all 7 letters, it's called a "Heptagram" worth an extra 7 points. See sample English game below.

Word Captain is now available in SEVEN languages: Danish, English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, and Swedish. See Swedish example below.
