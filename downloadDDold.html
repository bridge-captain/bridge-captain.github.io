11.23 This version includes a slight change in the underlying philosophy of the DD Bidding calculation. Consider the situation when your opponents land in an inferior contract. If you double them they may escape to a better contract. Formerly, the DD Bidding algorythm treated this as "We missed double" - since you failed to double a contract that should be defeated and we don't know for sure that they would have escape to a better contract. Henceforward the DD Bidding calculation will assume that they would have escaped to the better contract IF you were to double. Accordingly, in this situation this type of hand will be labeled "They missed better contract" instead of "We missed double."

Of course you will be able to continue to read in PBN and LIN files, but they will be converted immediately to SQL files. And - you can copy any SQL file back out to a PBN file if you want.
