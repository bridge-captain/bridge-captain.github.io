Setting up practice hands is a snap with Bridge Captain. In this example, I've clicked "Bergen Raises" from the "Select Dealing Pattern" drop-down box. Bridge Captain will continue to deal random hands similar to the strength and pattern shown below. Sometimes your suit will be spades, sometimes hearts. Sometimes your partner will have the opening hand, as shown below, sometimes you will. Sometimes the opponents will be able to interfere. What better way to practice.

There are over 60 different patterns from which to choose. Or you can set up your own unique pattern!
